# Health Data CSV Files

This directory contains mock health data for a single user over 30 days (November 2024).

## Files

### sleep.csv
Sleep tracking data including:
- Sleep duration, quality scores
- Deep sleep and REM sleep hours
- Bedtime and wake time
- Sleep efficiency, HRV, resting heart rate
- Number of awakenings

### nutrition.csv
Nutritional intake data including:
- Daily calories and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fats)
- Fiber, sugar, sodium intake
- Water consumption
- Meal breakdown (breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks)

### activity.csv
Physical activity data including:
- Steps, distance, calories burned
- Active and exercise minutes
- Workout types
- Heart rate data (avg, max, resting)
- VO2 max estimates

### vitals.csv
Vital signs and body metrics including:
- Weight, body fat percentage, muscle mass
- Resting heart rate, blood pressure
- HRV, body temperature
- Oxygen saturation
- Stress, energy, and mood levels

### wellness.csv
Wellness and lifestyle factors including:
- Stress, energy, mood, anxiety levels
- Productivity scores
- Social interactions
- Meditation time
- Outdoor time, screen time
- Alcohol, caffeine, smoking

## Data Patterns

The data includes realistic correlations:
- Poor sleep correlates with higher stress, lower energy, and increased sugar intake
- Low activity days correlate with higher stress and lower mood
- Good sleep nights correlate with better next-day performance
- Stress levels correlate with elevated heart rate and blood pressure

## Usage

These CSV files can be imported into your health aggregator application for:
- Data visualization
- Correlation analysis
- AI-powered insights
- Pattern detection
- Anomaly detection
